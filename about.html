About:

History

Scottish Housing News is the leading daily online news service for Scotland’s social and affordable housing sector.

Since its launch in 2001, SHN has grown to become a major resource for housing associations, local authorities and third sector organisations around the country and is read by more than 51,000 people each month.

51,000 readers / month

Our free daily newsletter and website is the ideal vehicle for advertisers who value cost-effectiveness, flexibility and the opportunity to access a highly-targeted audience.
